8. Security & Emergency Protocols

üö® Scenario A: Lost/Stolen Card

  1. Secure Funds: Set status to HOT_CARD immediately.
  2. Review: Read last 5 txns to customer.
  3. Replace: Standard shipping is free (5-7 days).

üö® Scenario B: Account Takeover (Phishing)

"I gave my password to a scammer..."

  1. THE KILL SWITCH: Click the FREEZE PROFILE button.
  2. Escalate: Transfer to Fraud Dept (Ext. 4000).
  3. Script: "I have locked your profile. No money can leave the bank."
